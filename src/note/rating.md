My [reviews](../../listening) focus on aspects that go beyond commercial success to the core of musical experience. That's why they are based on a set of personal criteria, reflecting an engagement with music as both an art form and a personal experience. Below is an outline of the the grading system and criteria I use to evaluate albums. It's not objective, and not meant to be.

## Grading System
My grading system ranges from 1 to 10, allowing for nuanced distinctions between albums:
- **9-10 (Exceptional)**: Albums that are outstanding in almost every regard, offering a profound musical experience.
- **7-8 (Great)**: Albums that are strong in most aspects, with considerable merit in their innovation, cohesion, or emotional impact.
- **5-6 (Good)**: Enjoyable albums with notable strengths but also some weaknesses or areas of inconsistency.
- **3-4 (Average)**: Albums that have a few good qualities but are largely unremarkable or lacking in key areas.
- **1-2 (Poor)**: Albums that fail to provide a meaningful or engaging listening experience, significantly lacking in several criteria.

## Review Criteria
1. **Emotional Resonance**: The capacity of the music to evoke emotions, with a higher score for albums that connect on a deeper emotional level. 
2. **Innovation and Originality**: Albums that introduce new ideas, sounds, or challenge genre boundaries receive higher scores.
3. **Cohesion and Flow**: The album's ability to present a unified narrative or mood throughout its duration, creating a seamless listening experience.
4. **Lyrical Depth**: The substance and impact of the album's lyrics, including storytelling, thematic depth, and poetic quality.
5. **Replay Value**: Albums that remain engaging and reveal new layers upon repeated listens score higher in this category.
6. **Personal Connection**: This subjective measure considers how the album relates to personal experiences or moments, recognizing that music's impact can vary greatly among listeners.
7. **Artistic Integrity**: The authenticity of the album and the sincerity of its artistic expression, valuing genuine creativity over commercial appeal.

### Application in Reviews
In each review, albums are evaluated based on the above criteria, with the final score reflecting a holistic view of the album's artistic value, emotional impact, and innovation. The goal is to provide a comprehensive analysis that aids in discovering music that resonates on a personal level, beyond mainstream appeal.
