title: Prevent Browser from Hijacking the Media Keys
updated: 2024-05-17
---
 Mindlessly you click a YouTube link and it starts playing. Surprise! Your play/pause button pauses the video instead of the music playing.

Easy fix, go to your Chromium-based browser and turn off Hardware Media Key Handling. Paste the following in the address bar: `chrome://flags/#hardware-media-key-handling`

There's also a third party application that fixes the same problem: [Reflex](https://stuntsoftware.com/reflex/). 

But I'm fine with turning off the media keys for the whole browser.
