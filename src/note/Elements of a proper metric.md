Setting good measures, for example for measuring [[AARM metrics]], or [[Business Outcomes, Product Outcomes, and Traction Metrics]], takes more than an objective and a number.

We'll need:

- **what** we’ll measure (the ‘scale’)
- **how** we’ll measure it (the ‘meter’)
- what the **current situation** is (the ‘benchmark’)
- the **minimum acceptable value** (e.g. *the break-even point*)
- for **investment** (the ‘constraint’)
- and the **desired value** (the ‘target’)
